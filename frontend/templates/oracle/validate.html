What Is Oracle Chain Validation?

Oracle Chain Validation connects healthcare actions to real-world legal frameworks, ensuring compliance with jurisdictional regulations. This process:

  • Validates your medical authority to perform specific actions based on your role and credentials
  • Verifies compliance with local and cross-border healthcare regulations
  • Generates cryptographic proof that the action has been properly validated
  • Creates an immutable audit trail for regulatory compliance

All validations are performed using zero-knowledge proofs, ensuring privacy while maintaining compliance.
